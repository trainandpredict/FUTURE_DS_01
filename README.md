# Sales Performance Dashboard Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyses business sales data to identify revenue trends, top-selling products, category performance, and regional sales performance. The goal is to provide business insights and recommendations to improve decision-making and growth.

## Objective

* Analyse revenue trends over time
* Identify top-selling products
* Determine high-value categories
* Evaluate regional sales performance
* Generate actionable business recommendations

## Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel (Data Cleaning & Preparation)
* Tableau (Dashboard Creation)
* GitHub (Project Documentation)

## Dataset

The dataset contains business sales records including product categories, sales, profit, quantity sold, and regional information.

## Dashboard Features

* Total Sales KPI
* Total Profit KPI
* Quantity Sold KPI
* Profit Ratio KPI
* Monthly Sales Trend Analysis
* Category Performance Analysis
* Sub-Category Performance Analysis
* City/Regional Performance Analysis

## Key Insights

1. Sales peaked during the final quarter of the year, especially in November.
2. Technology was the highest revenue-generating category.
3. Phones and Chairs were among the top-performing products.
4. New York generated the highest sales among all cities.
5. Some cities showed lower sales performance and may require targeted marketing efforts.

## Recommendations

* Increase inventory before peak sales periods.
* Focus marketing campaigns on high-performing categories such as Technology.
* Expand successful product lines like Phones and Chairs.
* Develop targeted strategies for underperforming regions.
* Monitor low-performing products and optimize inventory accordingly.
